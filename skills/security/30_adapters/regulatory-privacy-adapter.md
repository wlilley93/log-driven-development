# Regulatory Privacy Adapter

Apply when a request, code path, policy, evidence package, or customer response touches privacy law or regulated data obligations such as GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, HIPAA, DPAs, data subject rights, deletion/export, retention, subprocessors, or sensitive personal data.

## Required Checks

- Identify data classes and jurisdictions without giving final legal advice.
- Confirm implemented controls before making customer-facing claims.
- Map data rights requests to actual export, deletion, retention, and audit workflows.
- Check subprocessors, third-party tools, analytics, support logs, AI/tool calls, and backups for personal data exposure.
- Separate legal interpretation, which needs counsel, from technical evidence and control implementation.
- Record evidence gaps and owner for policy, product, or legal follow-up.
