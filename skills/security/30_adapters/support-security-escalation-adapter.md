# Support Security Escalation Adapter

Apply when a support, customer success, sales, or legal workflow receives a security, privacy, compliance, incident, access, data deletion/export, or confidentiality request.

## Required Checks

- Classify the request as security, privacy, compliance, legal, abuse, access, or procurement.
- Do not expose customer data, credentials, internal logs, privileged legal material, or security-sensitive implementation details in the response.
- Escalate suspected incidents, vulnerabilities, account compromise, data rights requests, and audit evidence requests to the owning workflow.
- Record what evidence was provided, what was withheld, and why.
- Ensure customer-facing claims match `privacy-governance-review` and `compliance-evidence-review`.
